Projects

Data Analytics Audit Project

This project was done for my Auditing (ACCTNG 405) course and involved collaboration with 5 of my peers. We were tasked with working as new members of a team that was auditing a hotel business as the client.

Our tasks included:

  • Researching how the client's operations, business risks, and opportunities might compare with the industry.
  • Working in a Tableau workbook to review the client's yearly financial reports.
  • Performing balance sheet, income statement, monthly account activity, dimension, date, gross margin, and other analyses in Tableau Desktop.
  • Making recommendations to help the client improve its business and manage risks.


Team Case Project on Data Centers

This project was done for my Information Management (MIS 402) course and involved collaboration with 4 of my peers. We were tasked with researching how companies are building efficient, reliable data centers, and presenting our findings and insights to our peers.

Our tasks included:

  • Researching industry practices regarding handling the growth and need for more data centers.
  • Posing critical thinking questions about data centers and providing solutions to possible problems with the development of data centers.
  • Creating a PowerPoint presentation to highlight our research, provide in-depth information about data center components, and solutions to problems posed.
