Education Background

I attended high school at St. Pius X, where I was involved in the National Honors Society. During my junior and senior years, I took college-level courses at Rockhurst University and the University of Missouri-Kansas City (UMKC). In 2020, I graduated from St. Pius X while earning the Bausch + Lomb Honorary Science Award from the University of Rochester.


In the Fall of 2020, I began my studies at the University of Missouri-Kansas City (UMKC) as a KC Scholar award recipient. Additionally, I was accepted into the Launchpad program at UMKC's Bloch School of Management.
